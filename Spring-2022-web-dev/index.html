MICRB Mission

Maryland Instructor Certification Review Board (MICRB) is an independent five-member board created as a unit within the University System of Maryland responsible for the administration of the State Emergency Services Instructor Certification Program.
Authority: Education Article, §§11-105 and 11-503, Annotated Code of Maryland - Title 13B Maryland Higher Education Commission, Subtitle 03 Fire-Rescue Education and Training Commission, Chapter 01 Certification Standards and Procedures for Emergency Services Instructors.

MICRB Site Purpose

Instructors and Sponsors will be able to locate MICRB-related questions and resources on this site with updated site enhanements to improve efficiency and efficacy of user experiences.
